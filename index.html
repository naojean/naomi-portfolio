Culture & Data

I am a firm believer in the notion that culture is the essence of marketing. From viral moments to subtle shifts in certain trends and aesthetics, it drives how people connect with brands. Through data, we can translate that cultural energy into marketing strategies: understanding trends, measuring sentiment, and identifying what truly resonates. I'm fascinated by how brands interpret this feedback loop in real-time, and how digital spaces constantly reshape what “relevance” means.
